Simon Says Game

How to Play:

  • The game will flash a color in a specific order.
  • Watch the sequence carefully and remember it.
  • After the sequence finishes, repeat it by clicking the colors in the correct order.
  • Each round, the sequence will get longer, making it harder.
  • If you click the wrong color, the game is over.
  • Your goal is to follow the longest sequence possible and set a high score!
